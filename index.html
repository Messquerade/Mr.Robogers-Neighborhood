Mr. Roboger's Neighborhood

Welcome to the neighborhood!

Everyone is Mr. Roboger's neighborhood gets a special message to welcome them to the neighborhood. Enter your favorite number to read your message.



Try all kinds of numbers!

Can you figure out the pattern? Depending on the number you enter, Mr. Roboger sends a different welcome message.

Mr. Roboger's favorite number is 3! He likes any number with a 3 in it. He also likes 1s and 2s.

If your number contains a 3, Mr. Roboger says, "Won't you be my neighbor?" no matter what! Next if it contains a 2, he says "Boop!" because he thinks it's a funny word. Any 1s in your number will get a "Beep!", because that's robot speak for "Welcome!". Any number that doesn't have a 1, 2, or 3 will get a range from 0 to your number with Beep! Boop! Won't you be my neighbor? at the very beginning!
